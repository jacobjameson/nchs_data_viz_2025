By Jacob Jameson — February 1, 2025

NHANES data reveals large gendered differences in depression severity


Among 2021-2023 NHANES survey participants who had received no mental treatment in the past year, women were 2x more likely than men to have a PHQ‑9 score that indicates severe depression.

A nine‑item depression screening questionnaire, called the Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ‑9), was administered to determine the frequency of depression symptoms over the past 2 weeks.
