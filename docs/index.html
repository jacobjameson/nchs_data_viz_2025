Data Shows Gender Disparities in
Depression and Suicide

By Jacob Jameson and Madison Coots — February 1, 2025

Extensive NHANES data reveals large differences in depression severity between genders. Explore the interactive visualizations below.

Women have higher depresion severity compared to Men


A nine‑item depression screening questionnaire, called the Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ‑9), was administered to determine the frequency of depression symptoms over the past 2 weeks.

Men
Women


Among 2021-2023 adult NHANES survey respondents, women were more than 3× more likely than men to have a PHQ‑9 score that indicates severe depression.
